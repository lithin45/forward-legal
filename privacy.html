Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version

Forward stores your workout data on your device. There is no account, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

What the app stores

Your exercises, workouts, sets, personal records, body-weight entries, and preferences are saved in a local database on your iPhone. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Apple Health

If you turn on “Sync to Apple Health”, Forward writes a workout record (activity type, start and end time) to Apple Health when you finish a session. Forward never reads data from Apple Health, and Health data is governed by Apple's privacy protections. You can revoke access anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

Notifications and alarms

The rest timer's Live Activity and optional rest alarm run entirely on your device through Apple's system frameworks. No notification content leaves your phone.

CSV import

Workout files you import from other apps are processed on-device and are not uploaded anywhere.

iCloud

This beta does not sync data to iCloud. If cloud sync ships in a future version, it will use your personal iCloud database, readable only by you, and this policy will be updated first.

Purchases

Forward is currently free and contains no third-party SDKs. If paid features are ever added, purchases would be handled by Apple and this policy will be updated first.
